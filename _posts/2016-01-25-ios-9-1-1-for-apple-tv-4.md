* This software update can be downloaded and applied to your Apple TV in the Settings → System → Softeare Updates section, right under the Maintenance heading.
* There, choose Update Software and then select the Download and Install option. When the update finishes downloading, select Update Now or Update Later. To update now and automatically install future updates, select Update Automatically.
* Don’t unplug your Apple TV while it restarts and installs the update.
