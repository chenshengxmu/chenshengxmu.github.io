While not yet available on Apple’s official YouTube channel, we have embedded it in this article so give it a quick watch and let us know how you liked it in the comments.
* The webpage has been reorganized around several common themes explaining how the Home app works, including features like device grouping, scenes, Siri support and remote control.

* Apple continues to advertise the fourth-generation Apple TV as a HomeKit hub which allows customers to control smart home devices in their house from just about anywhere.
* “Access your home remotely on your iOS device through Apple TV to do things like shut your garage door, view live camera video of the doorway, or even ask Siri to lower the thermostat—anything you normally do while using the Home app at home,” writes the company.
