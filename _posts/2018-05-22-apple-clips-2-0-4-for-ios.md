* The 21st FIFA World Cup is scheduled to take place in Russia from June 14 to July 15, 2018 so Apple’s conveniently updated its Clips video-editing software for iOS with related content.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Apple-Clips-2018-FIFA-World-Cup-update.png)
* New soccer-related content available in Clips 2.0.4 for iPhone, iPad and iPod touch includes soccer graphics and a label which you can add to your videos. Additionally, there’s a new soccer-themed background poster with customizable text elements.
* In March, Clips received another semi-major update bringing out freshly updated assets, including all-new selfie scenes, animated stickers and way more.

* Clips 2.0 came last November with impressive 360-degree selfie scenes that require the iPhone X TrueDepth camera, Star Wars stickers, a revamped user interface and other perks.
* With Clips, anyone can create personalized, expressive videos on their iOS device that combine videos, photos, music, filters, voice-based titles and other interactive elements.
* Clips is a free download from App Store.
