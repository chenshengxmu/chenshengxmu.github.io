title: Apple Maps public transit directions now available for Ireland
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/Apple-Maps-transit-directions-Ireland-Mac-screenshot-001.png)
* Apple customers in Ireland can now take advantage of live public transit directions in Apple Maps on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch and Mac.
* Using Apple Maps, customers can now plan their route to get from one point to another faster by using the country’s public transportation options such as bus and rail services, including the electric rail system called Dublin Area Rapid Transit.
* Ireland is the latest in a series of locations now offering transit directions, in addition to Taiwan and cities like Paris, Detroit, New Orleans and others recently being supported.
* Apple’s iOS Feature Availability webpage offers a more comprehensive list of cities offering transit directions around the globe.
* iOS 11’s improved Maps app also supports lane guidance and Do Not Disturb While Driving.
