title: Apple launches Spring-themed Apple Watch bands, Nike Sport straps now sold separately
---
* Apple today expanded its offering of first-party Apple Watch bands with all-new Spring-themed straps. Featuring a new Striped Woven Nylon band, among the other ones, the new bands are available to order starting today from apple.com and for purchase at select Apple Stores, Apple Authorized Resellers and carriers in late March.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Apple-Watch-Nylon-2017-band-002.jpg)
* Additionally, Nike Sport bands can now be purchased separately and the beautifully crafted Apple Watch Hermès bands are now available in striking new colors, too.
* Unfortunately, new Apple Watch purchases are now available with only two band options: Sport Band and Milanese Loop. Previously, you could buy your Apple Watch with band choices ranging from Classic Buckle, Leather Loop and Modern Buckle to Woven Nylon and Link Bracelet, but the leather bands are now sold separately.

* The Single Tour model starts at $339, the Double Tour model is $489 and the Double Buckle Cuff model is priced at $689.
* The new Apple Watch, Apple Watch Nike+ and Apple Watch Hermès models and the new bands will launch at Apple Galaxy Macau in late March. The new Apple Watch Hermès models and bands will be available at Hermès in Taipei Bellavita in late March.
