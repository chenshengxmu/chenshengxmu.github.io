title: New iPhone X ad highlights Portrait Mode selfies

![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Apple-Portrait-Selfie-Ad.jpg)
* Apple has shared a new video ad that highlights Portrait Mode selfies taken on the iPhone X utilizing Portrait Lighting effects. The short clip also includes a poem narrated by Muhammad Ali.
* The 38-second ad features a collection of images taken with the TrueDepth camera system of the iPhone X, as well as several different Portrait Lighting effects.
* The narration of the ad is an excerpt from Muhammed Ali’s spoken word album, I Am the Greatest.
* You can view m0re of Apple’s ads, or Portrait Mode tutorials on its YouTube page.
