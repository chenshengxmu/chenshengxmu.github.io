* Apple has released the fourth beta of iOS 7.1 to registered developers this morning. The update, which carries the build number 11D5134c, can be downloaded OTA or via Apple’s developer center.
* Today’s release comes just two weeks after the third beta, which brought about a number of UI changes including a new dialer, less vibrant icons for Messages, Phone and FaceTime apps, and more…
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/ios-7-beta-4.png)
* The beta is available for compatible iPhone, iPad and iPod touch models. We’re installing it ourselves, and will be reporting back with any new stuff we find. So make sure that you check back with us.
* And as always, we welcome you to let us know if you find anything that we may have missed by either e-mailing us at tips@idownloadblog.com, or leaving us a comment on the change or changes below.
* Today’s iOS 7.1 beta 4 (11D5134c) released to developers expires on Monday, Monday March 3, 2014 @ 12AM (GMT). #TheMoreYouKnow
