title: How to delete previously-used Apple IDs and emails on Apple TV
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Previously-Used-Emails.jpg)
* When you use your Apple TV, you likely sign in with your Apple ID to download apps from the App Store and log in to certain apps with your email address. These IDs and emails are stored so that you can easily reuse them. But, what if you want to remove one?
* Here’s how to delete Apple IDs and email addresses on Apple TV.
* 1) Open the Settings app.
* 2) Click Accounts.
* 3) Select iTunes and App Store.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Accounts.jpg)
* On the next screen you have an option to add a new Apple ID. Right below that, you can see those Apple IDs stored.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Remove-ID-Trash-Can.jpg)
* 1) To remove an Apple ID, click the trash can icon next to it.
* 2) When prompted to confirm the deletion, click Delete.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Confirm-Delete-ID.jpg)
* 3) On the iTunes and App Store screen, the Apple ID should be gone. Click your Menu button to exit the screen and go back to your settings.
* 1) Open the Settings app.
* 2) Click General.
* 3) Scroll down to and select Previously-Used Emails.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Previously-Used-Emails.jpg)
* 4) Click the Edit button at the top to delete one email at a time.
* 5) Click the trash can icon next to the email you want to delete.
* 6) When prompted to confirm the deletion, click Remove.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Confirm-Remove-Email.jpg)
* 7) Click Done when you finish removing emails and then your Menu button to exit the screen and go back to your settings.
* If you want to remove all email addresses in the list, simply click Remove All when you arrive on the screen and then when prompted to confirm the deletion, click Remove.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Apple-TV-Email-List.jpg)
