![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/iPhone-Settings-Icon-Banner.jpg)
* System Info is a new free jailbreak tweak that augments the Settings → General → About page on your iPhone or iPad. It adds a bevy of supplementary information to make the page more enlightening, but it also integrates some distinct new features that many might find useful.
* For one, you can now copy information directly from the page, such as your UDID, IMEI, etc. Moreover, you can now get brief descriptions of many of the confusing readouts that you might not understand. Last but not least, System Info includes a way to save your device’s SHSH files locally on your mobile device.
* To learn more about how the tweak works and why you should download it today, we’d recommend reading our full review, which goes over all the details.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/UnsplashWalls-Wallpapers.jpg)
* You undoubtedly get tired of the same old wallpaper day in and day out, but choosing a new wallpaper that actually looks good can be a challenging task. UnsplashWalls is a new jailbreak tweak that aims to change that.
* This ultimately lets you change your wallpaper to a random image from the Unsplash stock image website on demand. You can assign an Activator gesture to change your wallpaper on the fly anytime you get tired of the one you’re already using.
