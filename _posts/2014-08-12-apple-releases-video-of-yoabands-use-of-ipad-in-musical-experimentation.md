* Following yesterday’s release of two new ‘Your Verse’ stories from Apple, the Cupertino giant has made an accompanying video to one of the stories available on its YouTube channel. The clip showcases Yaoband, a China-based music band that was featured in one of the two recent iPad stories, as they demonstrate ways in which Apple’s tablet is used in their music creation process…
* https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I8e9HYsInAw
* Part of Apple’s “Your Verse” campaign, which shows how people around the world use Apple devices to work, play, and create, this new video is a two minute, fifteen second version of one of the many videos in its category. Focusing on how the band records everyday sounds with an iPad mini and then turns them into percussion tones, the video follows Yaoband everywhere from the back of their van to the live stage.
