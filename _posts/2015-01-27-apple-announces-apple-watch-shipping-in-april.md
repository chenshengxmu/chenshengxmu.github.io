title: Apple announces Apple Watch shipping in April
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Apple-Watch-Cult-of-Mac-001.jpg)
* Apple CEO Tim Cook just shed more light on the Apple Watch’s launch this spring, announcing on the company’s quarterly earnings call the Apple Watch will be shipping to customers in April.
* Cook didn’t provide a specific launch date during April, saying development was on track. However he did dispel the rumor the Apple Watch would launch in March.
* When the Apple Watch was announced in September, the company only said it was targeting a launch for early 2015.
* The company still hasn’t provided details on pricing.
