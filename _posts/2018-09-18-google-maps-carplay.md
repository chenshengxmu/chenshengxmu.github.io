* Google Maps is officially supported by Apple CarPlay. Thanks to this support, iPhone users can now navigate with Google Maps from their car’s built-in display.
* With Google Maps on CarPlay, you can use the same navigation experience found in the iOS app. This includes the ability to search for places, see alternative routes, and get live, up-to-date information about traffic issues and delays. You can also see an up-to-the-minute ETA, so you’ll know how long it will take to get to your location.
* Thanks to the integration, you can start Google Maps on your iPhone and continue through CarPlay once you’ve connected. This is especially valuable when every minute counts.
