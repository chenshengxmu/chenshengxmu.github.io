* Fans of the popular Monument Valley mobile games who happen to live in the Android ecosystem will be happy to hear that Monument Valley 2 will be coming to Google’s mobile OS at the beginning of November.
* Monument Valley and its sequel have been two of the most popular and critically acclaimed mobile games of all time, and it is undoubtedly exciting that the users of the world’s most used mobile OS can finally get their hands on another great game from ustwo Games Ltd.
* In case you have not had a chance to play them, Monument Valley and its sequel are gorgeous puzzle games that defy common reason in delightfully interesting ways. You can download Monument Valley 2 for $4.99 and the original Monument Valley for $3.99.
