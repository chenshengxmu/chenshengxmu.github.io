* Each Sunday, the Wallpapers of the Week collection strives to bring you incredible, photo-quality images for your iOS devices. These posts are made possible by gathering reader submissions, stumbling across great content on the web, and closely watching what comes across on social media. This week we highlight a very talented iDB reader who submitted an original photography set of ten iPhone wallpapers.
* Below is a set of original photographs by @solefield. She is not new to being featured in our wallpapers category, but this is the first collection of wallpapers she has made with the iPhone X as the inspiration. If you like what you see below, you can follow her on Instagram @solefield.photography or check out her personal dedicated gallery.
* All of the following images will be suited for any iPhone device, just keep in mind you will need to zoom a little, given the aspect ratio of the iPhone X is slightly more narrow that previous generations.
* If you have a great wallpaper or a whole collection, catch up with me on Twitter @jim_gresham, where I curate each week’s images. If you follow along, you just might find some mid-week downloads too. At the least, you will be the first to see a preview of the upcoming week’s posts.
