title: How to disable suggested places for Siri and Spotlight Search on iPhone and iPad
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Siri-suggestions-locations.jpg)
* Siri and Spotlight Search are way more useful in iOS with suggestions that update results as you type. In addition to relevant apps and people, proactive suggestions also include nearby places such as restaurants, gas stations, retail stores and other points of interest.
* Should you find place suggestions unappealing and potentially impacting your privacy, you can turn them off with just a few taps.
* In this post, you will learn how to disable suggested location in Siri and Spotlight Search on your iPhone, iPod touch or iPad with iOS 9 or later.
* Step 1: Open Settings on your iOS device.
* Step 2: Go to the Privacy → Location Services section.
* Step 3: Now scroll down and tap System Services at the very bottom.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/How-to-disable-suggested-locations-in-Siri-and-Spotlight-iPhone-screenshot-003.png)
* Step 4: Flip the switch next to Location-Based Suggestions to the OFF position.
* In addition to nearby places, Siri’s proactive suggestions include relevant apps that you launch at specific times of the day, people you tend to contact at specific times, news and other items. Siri Suggestions are supported on the iPhone 5 and later, iPad Pro, fourth-generation iPad and later, second-generation iPad mini and later and sixth-generation iPod touch.
