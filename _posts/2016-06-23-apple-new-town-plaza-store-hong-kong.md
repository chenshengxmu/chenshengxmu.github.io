title: Grand opening of fifth Apple Store in Hong Kong scheduled for June 30
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Apple-Store-Hong-Kong-New-Town-Plaza-image-001.jpg)
* Hong Kong, a special administrative region of the People’s Republic of China, will get its fifth Apple retail store a week from today on Thursday, June 30, Apple announced today. Located inside the New Town Plaza shopping mall at 18 Sha Tin Centre Street, the store will be open seven days a week between 10:00am to 10pm local time.
* Apple’s new retail location in Hong Kong will offer the Genius Bar along with other expected services, store events, workshops and more.
* The New Town Plaza outlet is Apple’s 46th retail store in Greater China, in addition to its Canton Road, Causeway Bay, Festival Walk and IFC Mall stores in the region.
* Source: Apple Retail via MacRumors
