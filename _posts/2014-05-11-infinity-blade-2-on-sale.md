* Good news gamers, Chair Entertainment has dropped the price on its popular title Infinity Blade II. For a limited time, you can pick up the game, which normally retails for $6.99, for just $0.99 for both the iPhone and iPad versions.
* Infinity Blade II was first announced in October of 2011, during the iPhone 4S event, and was released on December 1 of that year. It’s powered by Epic’s Unreal Engine 3 tech, and it features the same addictive gameplay as the original…
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/infinity-blade-21.jpg)
* From the App Store description:
* “The God King has been defeated, an unlikely hero has emerged and now you must discover the truth behind the secrets of the Infinity Blade. The continuing journey of young Siris unfolds as you delve deeper into the world of the Deathless tyrants and their legion of Titans. Can you unlock all the mysteries and successfully wield the power of the Infinity Blade in this timeless swordplay adventure of champions and villains?”

* If you’re still not convinced that the game is good enough to spend $1, it’s worth mentioning that Infinity Blade II received over 15 perfect scores and more than 20 Game of the Year Awards from various, accredited gaming publications.
