* iPhone X Wallpaper Pack 3 is the continuation of our Wallpapers of the Week exclusive mini series! Before rushing to download today’s wallpaper set, make sure you don’t miss Pack 1 and Pack 2. We could not be more excited for the edge-to-edge OLED iPhone screen. A full 5.8″ gorgeous display will need amazing wallpapers and we are here with an exclusive pack of images, just waiting to be synced to your new device. Start collecting now!
* Answering the Call to Action, aspiring designer and artists @danielghuffman, scoured the unsplash.com database for excellent wallpaper posts. While the images were not originally listed as iPhone-sized wallpapers, Daniel was able to estimate a size of 1,750 x 3,000, given earlier iOS leak information. The new iPhone X screen size is smaller, but having a larger image will help with parallax effects and give a little ability to re-size this when setting the image. As you can see above, the wallpapers look amazing on the iPhone X body type.
* We appreciate the quick work of @danielghuffman and for taking time to make not, just this pack, but iPhone X Wallpaper Pack 2 as well! An aspiring artist and designer, he has a load of cool images on his own online gallery.
* If you find or create iPhone X wallpapers or want need to be alerted when we come across more, just follow along via @jim_gresham. On Twitter, I communicate with Wallpaper of the Week fans, collect submissions, and tweet out images throughout the week! Please help your iDB wallpaper neighbors and send over any iPhone X wallpapers for featuring on the site.
