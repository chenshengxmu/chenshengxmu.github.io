![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/HomePod-order-receipt.png)
* Apple’s HomePod is now available for pre-order in the US, UK, and Australia with the first deliveries to customers scheduled for February 9th.
* Available in either White or Space Gray, HomePod is priced at $349 in the US, £319 in the UK, and AUS$499 in Australia.
* These three countries are the only places where HomePod is available at launch, with Germany and France being added to the list this Spring.
