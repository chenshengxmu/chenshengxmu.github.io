* On Wednesday, September 12, Apple announced the iPhone Xs, Xs Max, and Xr. As always, Apple included amazing advertising wallpapers to showcase the devices in the hands on area after the presentation and also on the website.
* With some quick handiwork from both @AR72014 and @Sniper_GER, the images have been grabbed from the official advertising imagery. You don’t have to wait until next Friday or even order a device to grab the entire series of iPhone Xs wallpapers or iPhone Xr wallpapers. Maybe someone will think you already have one of the new Xs models, given the form factor is exactly the same.
* If you are regularly looking for incredible wallpapers for your iOS devices, then you should watch out for our Wallpapers of the Week post, which hits every Sunday. Like this iPhone Xr and Xs wallpaper collection, the section is curated by images submitted to me via @jim_gresham. If you have any awesome wallpapers, tips, or tricks, catch up with me on Twitter.
