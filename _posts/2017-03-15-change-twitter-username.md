* Your Twitter username, or Twitter handle, is basically your identity on the social network. This is how people can find you and send you messages, either with a public mention or via DM. As you grow with the times, you may come to a point where you want to change your existing username to something new.
* Fortunately, Twitter makes it very easy for you to change your Twitter handle, and in this tutorial we’ll show you how to change it.
* This process can be performed in a web browser on your computer, or in the mobile app on your iOS device(s). Neither way is very time-consuming, and we’ll show you the process for each in this piece.
* If you’re using the free Twitter app from the App Store for this tutorial, then follow these steps to change your Twitter username:
* 1) Launch the Twitter app from your Home screen.
* 2) Tap on the Me tab at the bottom of the app.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Twitter-Account-Settings-Name-Change-1.jpg)
* 3) Tap on the Settings button on the right side of your profile.
* 4) Tap on the Settings option from the pop-up menu list.
* 5) Tap on the Account option from the menu list.
* 6) Tap on the Username cell.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Twitter-Account-Settings-Change-Name-2.jpg)
* 7) Tap inside the New text field and type in a new username that you desire to have.
* 8) Tap on the Done button at the top right of the app to save your changes.
* 9) Tap on the Done button at the top right of the app again to exit the settings.
* You’re all done! Your account should now have its new handle.
* If you’re using a web browser for this tutorial, then follow these steps to change your Twitter username:
* 1) Launch your favorite web browser and sign into Twitter.
* 2) Click on the Profile button at the top right of the page.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Change-Twitter-Username-Web-1.jpg)
* 3) Click on the Settings and Privacy option from the drop-down menu.
* 4) Under the Account section, type a new username in the Username text field.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Change-Twitter-Username-Web-2.jpg)
* 5) Scroll down and click on the blue Save changes button.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Change-Twitter-Username-Web-3.jpg)
* 6) Twitter asks you to enter your password to verify your identity; enter it, and then click on the blue Save button.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Twitter-Change-Username-Save-Changes-Button.jpg)
* 7) Twitter should now show you that your changes have been saved:
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Change-Twitter-Username-Saved-Changes.jpg)
* And that’s all there is to it!
