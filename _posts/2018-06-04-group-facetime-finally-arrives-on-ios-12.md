title: Group FaceTime finally arrives on iOS 12

![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wwdc18-547.jpg)
* Apple has finally added Group FaceTime in iOS 12. You can add up to 32 people to one call. Yes, 32! Better still, Group FaceTime can also be added to a Group Message with ease.
* Everyone on the call can also use the recently announce emoji updates too. It works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. And you can also answer via your Apple Watch.
