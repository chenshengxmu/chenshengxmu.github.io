* Keeping true to its promise, Apple has now officially added more than a hundred new emoji to the second betas for iOS 11.1, watchOS 4.1 and macOS High Sierra 10.13.1.
* The updates were seeded to developers and public beta testers today.
* Among the new emoji are such characters as Crazy Face, Shushing Face, Vampire, T-Rex, Flying Saucer, Broccoli and others. You’ll also find more emotive smiley faces, as well as new gender-neutral characters, clothing options, food types, animals, mythical creatures and more.
* Many of the new emoji include multiple skin tones and gender options.
