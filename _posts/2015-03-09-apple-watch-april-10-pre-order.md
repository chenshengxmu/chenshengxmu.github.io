title: Apple Watch will be available for pre-order April 10, ships on April 24
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Apple-Watch-pre-order.png)
* Wrapping up the Apple Watch portion of today’s event, Tim Cook answered a number of long-awaited questions regarding the upcoming wearable. We now know more about pricing, configurations, and when it’s going to be available. Cook says the Apple Watch will become eligible for pre-order on April 10, and will start shipping on April 24.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Apple-Watch-countries.png)
* Apple Watch will be available in the above countries on April 24, with a starting price of $349 for the 38mm Sports model and $399 for the 42mm. Likewise, the standard Apple Watch model will start at $549, and $50 more for the 42mm face. And if you have money to burn, pricing for the gold Edition model will star at a staggering $10,000.
