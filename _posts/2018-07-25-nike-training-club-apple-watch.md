* The Nike Training Club app is ready to push your exercise routines to new levels thanks to the all-new Apple Watch integration. Called your “Ultimate Personal Trainer,” the app offers over 180 free workouts from strength and endurance to mobility and yoga. Whether you’re a seasoned athlete or just getting started, Nike believes this is the app for you. 
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/nike-workout-club.jpg)
* Available for free on the App Store, the Nike Training Club app offers personalized training plans that adapt to you and your schedule. It also includes daily “Picks For You” workouts that are based on your routine. Not surprisingly, the more you train, the more personalized your recommendations are supposed to become.
* With Apple Watch support, the Nike Training Club app allows you to control your workout without needing your iPhone. In doing so, you can advance to the next drill, pause, skill, and more, from your wrist all while monitoring your heart rate and calories.
