* Apple has made the third betas of upcoming iOS 11.3 and macOS High Sierra 10.13.4 updates available to public testers. Those in the Beta Software Program can download the updates from Apple’s website, or via their respective over-the-air update mechanisms.

* As for macOS High Sierra 10.13.4, Apple says it includes bug fixes and performance improvements, as well as support for new iOS 11.3 features like Messages on iCloud and Business Chat, but not much else. Wallpaper fans, however, will be happy to hear it does include the smoky iMac Pro background.
