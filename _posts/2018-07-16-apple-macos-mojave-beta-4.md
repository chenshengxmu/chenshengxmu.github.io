title: Apple releases macOS Mojave beta 4 to developers
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/macos-mojave.jpg)
* On Monday, Apple released the fourth macOS Mojave beta to developers. 
* The latest beta update fixes a bug that prevented Dynamic Desktop from working while in Dark Mode and also introduces a new Solar Gradient dynamic wallpaper option.
* First announced in June, macOS Mojave 10.14 includes lots of new features including Stacks for organizing files on your desktop, a new Gallery view and Quick Actions in Find, iOS apps now on Mac, including News, Stocks, Home, and Voice Memos, a new Mac App Store and more.
* Apple’s expected to launch macOS Mojave to the public this fall along with next-generation versions of iOS, tvOS, and watchOS.
