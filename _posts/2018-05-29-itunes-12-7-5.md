![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/itunes-12.7.5-upate-prompt.jpg)
* Alongside iOS 11.4, tvOS 11.4, watchOS 4.3.1 and all-new HomePod software, Apple today bumped iTunes to version 12.7.5, a minor update with under-the-hood improvements.
* ”This update includes minor app and performance improvements,” according to the changelog.
* To deploy the update on your Mac computer, fire up the Mac App Store app, click the Updates tab, then click the Install button next to the listed iTunes 12.7.5 update.
* Alternatively, select Check for Updates from the iTunes menu.
* iTunes for Mac and Windows can be downloaded from Apple.com.
