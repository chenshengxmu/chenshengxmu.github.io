title: Minimal action game Super Hexagon goes free within Apple Store shopping app
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Super-Hexagon-1.3-for-iOS-iPhone-screenshot-002.jpeg)
* Apple is giving away free copies of Super Hexagon, a minimal action game by Terry Cavanagh, with music by Chipzel. This nicely done iPhone and iPad game is being provided as a free download exclusively through the official Apple Store shopping app.
* “Be challenged by the difficult yet beautiful gameplay of Super Hexagon,” writes Apple. “Survive as long as you can through endless levels of minimalist puzzles.”
* Super Hexagon has a rating of 4.5 out of 5 stars based on submissions from nearly 9,000 App Store reviewers. The game currently sells for $2.99 in the App Store so make sure to grab it at no charge through the official Apple Store shopping app, here’s how.
* Step 1: Download Apple’s free Apple Store app to your iPhone, iPod touch or iPad.
* Step 2: Open the app on your iPhone or iPod touch.
* Step 3: Tap on the Stores tab at the bottom of the screen.
* Step 4: Now swipe on the carousel right below the One to One option until you see teaser graphics for Super Hexagon.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Super-Hexagon-1.3-for-iOS-how-to-redeem-001.png)
* Tap it to get started.
* Step 5: On the next screen tap the green “Download now for free” button at the bottom.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Super-Hexagon-1.3-for-iOS-how-to-redeem-002.png)
* Step 6: You’ll be taken to the App Store app to confirm your Apple ID password.
* Step 7: Lastly, tap the Redeem button in the upper right corner of the screen and the game will begin downloading to your device.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Super-Hexagon-1.3-for-iOS-how-to-redeem-003.png)
* Of course, you can re-download Super Hexagon at any time to your other devices through the App Store’s Purchased tab.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Super-Hexagon-1.3-for-iOS-iPhone-screenshot-003.jpeg)
* This offer is exclusive for users of the Apple Store app and expires on October 30, 2015.
* Apple says the promotion is valid only for US App Store account holders but that’s not a major pain given it’s easy to create a US account with the App Store alongside your existing account.
* Tip: If you cannot access the offer and are located outside the United States, disabling location access in the Apple Store section of the Settings app should do the trick.
* If you don’t see the embedded video, watch it on YouTube.
* For more information about the game, see superhexagon.com.
* Super Hexagon is available on iOS, Android, Mac, Windows and Linux platforms. Cross-platform Mac, Windows and Linux versions are DRM-free and provided as a single $2.99 purchase through the official website.
* On the iOS platform, the 22-megabyte game requires an iPhone, iPod touch or iPad with iOS 6.1 or later.
* Super Hexagon is localized in English, Czech, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, Swedish, Traditional Chinese and Turkish.
* The game sells for $2.99 in the App Store.
