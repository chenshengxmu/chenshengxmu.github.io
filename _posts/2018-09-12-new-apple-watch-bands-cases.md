title: Apple debuts new cases for the iPhone Xs and Xs Max, new Apple Watch bands
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/case.png)
* Apple on Wednesday debuted a slew of new accessories to go with its new iPhone and Apple Watch models. Among the accessories are new cases for the iPhone Xs and Xs Max, as well as new bands for the just-announced Apple Watch Series 4.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/case2.png)
* The new silicone cases are priced, as usual, at $39 for both the iPhone Xs and Xs Max and come in several colors. There are also new Leather Cases for both handsets, priced at $49, and Folio Cases hat cost $99 for the Xs and $129 for the Xs Max.
* As for the Apple Watch bands, it’s mostly new colorways for existing lines. Sport Band has a few new colors, and will run you $49. Same with the Sport Loop. The Modern Buckle and Leather Loop cost $149, and also add a handful of new colors.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/bands.png)
* Finally, for those with more expensive tastes, there’s a new Gold Milanese Loop band for $149 and new Hermès bands that cost $339 for the Single Tour and $489 for Double Tour. And of course, all of these are available now on Apple’s website.
