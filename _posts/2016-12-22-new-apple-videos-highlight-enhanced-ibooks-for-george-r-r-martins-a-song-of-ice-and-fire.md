* Back in September, publisher HarperCollins debuted on iBooks Store exclusive Game of Thrones digital books featuring interactive content. Today, Apple began promoting these enhanced edition iBooks in last-minute holiday push with a trio of new videos published on its YouTube channel.
* Comprised of five individual titles, these interactive iBook exclusives are available individually or as a $39.99 bundle from iBooks Store.
* The enhanced edition iBooks of George R. R. Martin’s Game of Thrones series let you navigate the astounding world of Westeros with interactive maps, author notes, family trees and illustrations that add to the adventure.
* “Explore the ultimate edition of Book 2 in George R. R. Martin’s epic fantasy that began with A Game of Thrones,” Apple wrote in the videos’ description.
* A Game of Thrones: Enhanced Edition—The Life of the Imagination
* A Clash of Kings: Enhanced Edition—Hard Choices
* A Storm of Swords: Enhanced Edition—Family Ties
