title: Amazon rebrands Cloud Drive Photos app, adds iPhone 6 support and other new features
---
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Amazon-Cloud-Drive-Photos-3.0-for-iOS-iPhone-screenshot-002.jpeg)
* Amazon is out today with a major refresh to its Cloud Drive Photos app for iOS, which now has a new name, a freshly revamped icon and half a dozen new features.
* Known henceforth as just Amazon Photo, the updated software includes full support for the new 4.7″ and 5.5″ screen resolutions of the iPhone 6 and 6 Plus, respectively.
* It also does duplicate photo detection, lets you upload your images to the company’s cloud storage service in full resolution, allows you to report issues directly through the app and more.
* Check out the old icon at left and the new at right.
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Amazon-Cloud-Drive-Photos-3.0-for-iOS-app-icon-small.png)
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Amazon-Photo-3.3-for-iOS-app-icon-small.png)
* So, in addition to support for the new handsets and full-res upload capability, the app also boasts an improved landscape mode on iPads and a duplicate detection feature which will issue a warning if the photo being uploaded already exists in the cloud.
