* Aside from nearly two-dozen bug and security fixes and various under-the-hood improvements, iOS 11.2 brings support for the faster 7.5W wireless charging on iPhone 8, iPhone 8 Plus and iPhone X and the Apple Pay Cash peer-to-peer payment feature.
* Released on December 2, 2017, the iOS 11.2 software update is compatible with any iPhone model from iPhone 5s onward, including iPad Air and later and the sixth-generation iPod touch.
* To update your device to the latest available version of iOS over the air, go to Settings → General → Software Update and follow the onscreen instructions. You can also apply the update using your computer: connect your device to a Mac or Windows PC, select it in iTunes, then select the Summary tab and click the Check for Update button.
