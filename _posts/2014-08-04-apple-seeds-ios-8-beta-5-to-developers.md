title: Apple seeds iOS 8 beta 5 to developers- here is what's new

![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/iOS-8-beta-5.png)
* Coming two weeks after the last iOS beta, which contained several visual tweaks and changes to Settings.app, Apple has released iOS 8 beta 5 build 12A4345d to developers. Besides the usual mix of bug fixes and improvements, there are likely to be multiple UI tweaks and other noticeable changes. Apple has more details in beta 5’s release notes.
* We’re currently installing the beta and will be updating this post with any changes we find. If you find any changes from the previous beta, be sure to email us at tips@idownloadblog.com or leave a comment below, preferably with a screenshot of the modification…
* New in iOS 8 beta 5:
* New sound after a voice message is sent in Messages
* Home Data renamed to HomeKit in Settings > Privacy
* Minor sorting method change in Settings > General > Restrictions
* Spirometry data types have been added to HealthKit
* Swiping down to open Spotlight is much faster
* New Spotlight blur on older devices
* Brightness controls have been removed from Wallpaper settings panel
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Brightness-removed-Wallpaper.jpg)
* Privacy settings for Health
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Health-privacy-settings.png)
* “T-Mobile Wi-Fi” carrier name
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/t-mobile-wifi-carrier.png)
* New icons for iCloud, iCloud Family Sharing, iCloud Drive, Backup, and Keychain
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/new-iCloud-icon.jpg)
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/new-iCloud-services-icons.png)
* Continuity SMS sync now called SMS Relay, can be enabled/disabled
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/SMS-relay-prompt.png)
* Option to Export Health Data in Health.app
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/export-Health-data.jpg)
* “Last Updated” information for iCloud sync in Photos
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Photos-iCloud-last-updated.jpg)
* “Predictive” toggle in the keyboard switcher
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/keyboard-Predictive-toggle.jpg)
* Option to Optimize iPhone Storage or Download and Keep Originals, Pause for 1 Day option removed
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Photos-Camera-iOS-8-beta-5.png)
* Photos app now displays the location where the photo was taken above the photo
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Photos-location.jpg)
* iOS restore screen now uses a red iTunes icon
![img](http://media.idownloadblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/red-iTunes-icon-restore.jpg)
