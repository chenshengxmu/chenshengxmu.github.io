* With the launch of WatchOS 2, Apple Watch now has the ability to include user-selected wallpapers on select watch faces. Either a photo album will rotate through wallpapers each time the watch lights up or a single photo may be chosen.
* Now, customization is more of a possibility and matching device wallpapers can create a nice effect. Thanks to @AR72014, this Wallpaper of the Week post includes optimized Apple Watch wallpapers from previous versions of iOS, including iOS 9. Take a step past the fold to find your favorites, both new and old. 
* The first set includes previous iOS generation wallpapers, specifically tailored for Apple Watch. Below is a list of 51 wallpapers. You can download them individually, or visit AR7’s Deviant Art page to download the Zip file for all of them. Remember, the photos need to be in a dedicated album on your iPhone to sync, so choose your download method appropriately.
* Previous iOS Apple Watch Wallpapers: Zip download via Deviant Art
